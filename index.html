Poets and Pronouns

We are an informal reading group focusing on syntax of pronouns and related topics. Meetings take place at 6:30 on Thursday, at The Poet and Patriot, unless noted otherwise. Handouts and beer are mandatory.

To contact the organizers, please email ddbrodki[at]ucsc.edu or vsunilar[at]ucsc.edu.
