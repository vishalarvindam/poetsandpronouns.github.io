Poets and Pronouns

We are an informal reading group comprised of linguists at UC Santa Cruz focusing on the syntax of pronouns and related topics. Usually, meetings take place at The Poet and Patriot, but we will be moving online for the foreseeable future. Handouts (and drinks) are encouraged. This group is rent burdened and supports the COLA Campaign.

To contact the organizers, please email Dan Brodkin at ddbrodki[at]ucsc.edu or Vishal Arvindam vsunilar[at]ucsc.edu.
